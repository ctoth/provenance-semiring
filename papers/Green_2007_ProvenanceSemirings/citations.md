## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Buneman, Khanna, Tan (2001) [4]**: Foundational definition of why-provenance and where-provenance that this paper unifies under the semiring framework. Already in collection as `Buneman_2001_CharacterizationDataProvenance`.
- **Cui, Widom (2000, 2003) [9, 10]**: Original lineage definition for data warehousing — the other major provenance notion unified by this paper.
- **Imielinski, Lipski (1984) [17]**: Conditional tables for incomplete databases — the semiring framework generalizes this classical approach.
- **Fuhr, Rolleke (1997) [14]**: Probabilistic relational algebra — shows how probability computation is a special case of semiring provenance.
- **Davey, Priestley (2002) [11]**: Lattice theory reference — needed for understanding the query containment results over distributive lattices.
